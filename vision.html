VISION

Catalyzing Swachh Bharat Mission through innovative young minds.

Challenges

Government administrators are crunched for time as they have hundreds of things on their daily agenda. Every district has its set of core problem areas and the administrator has to focus his attention to those areas, most of which are not sanitation related. Zila Swachh Bharat Prerak is essentially a ‘Fellowship’ which will offer a unique opportunity to fellows to partner with leaders in government ranging from District Collectors to District Development Officers in order to implement a high impact and scalable project. The Fellowship seeks to bring together experienced government leaders with young, enterprising future leaders to use the power of data, motivation and enthusiasm to solve one of India's most critical problems of Open Defecation and sanitation.
