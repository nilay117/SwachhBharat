ABOUT US

THE ORGANISATION

Tata Trusts is India's leading philanthropic organization and has played a pioneering role in transforming traditional ideas of charity to philanthropy that makes a sustainable difference to marginalised communities. Through grant-making, direct implementation and collaborative partnerships, The Tata Trusts are driving impact in areas including arts, crafts and culture; natural resources management; education; healthcare and nutrition; and rural livelihoods. Spread over 17 states and 170 districts across the country, programmes supported by Tata Trusts reach out to millions of households through an efficient network of 450 plus partner organizations.

SWACH BHARAT MISSION (GRAMIN)

To accelerate the efforts towards achieving universal sanitation coverage and to focus on sanitation, the Honourable Prime Minister of India, Shri Narendra Modi, launched the Swachh Bharat Mission on October 2, 2014. The Swachh Bharat Mission comprises of two Sub-Missions, the Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin) and the Swachh Bharat Mission (Urban). The Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin) aims to achieve an Open Defecation Free India and improved the levels of cleanliness in rural areas by 2019.

To support the Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin), the Tata Trusts intends to recruit motivated Young Professionals as Zila Swachh Bharat Preraks (ZSBPs) and depute them at the District level to work directly with the District Collector or the Chief Executive Officer (Zila Panchayat) or the District Development Officer. The ZSBPs will facilitate the implementation of SBM-G in the District.

The ZSBP programme is a joint initiative of the Ministry of Drinking Water and Sanitation, Government of India and the Tata Trusts that is being implemented in collaboration with the State Governments. The programme is focusing on achieving Open Defecation Free (ODF) status of all villages and on improving the sanitation and cleanliness levels of villages.

The Zila Swachh Bharat Prerak initiative is a unique opportunity for Young Professionals to contribute to nation building and work on such a high impact project as the Swachh Bharat Mission. Aimed at bringing about positive change through structured integration of youth, this is a first of its kind partnership between a philanthropic organization and Government of India to effect change at this scale.
